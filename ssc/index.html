❓ SSC Mock Test — FAQ

Common questions about SSC exams and mock tests

SSC CGL Tier-1 mein 100 questions aate hain 60 minutes mein. 4 sections hote hain: Quantitative Aptitude (25), General Intelligence & Reasoning (25), English Language (25), aur General Awareness (25). Har correct answer ke 2 marks milte hain aur har galat answer pe 0.50 marks katate hain.
Dono ka Tier-1 pattern same hai (100 Qs, 60 min), lekin CGL Graduate level posts ke liye hai (Inspector, Auditor, etc.) jabki CHSL 12th pass candidates ke liye hai (LDC, DEO, etc.). CGL ka difficulty level thoda zyada hota hai.
SSC CGL, CHSL, aur GD mein -0.50 per wrong answer hota hai (2 marks per correct ke saath). SSC MTS, CPO, Steno, JE mein -0.25 hota hai (1 mark per correct ke saath). Isliye guess karne se pehle sochein!

SSC 2026 ke tentative dates: CGL Tier-1 (June-July 2026), CHSL Tier-1 (August-September 2026), MTS (October 2026), GD Constable (November-December 2026). Official notification SSC ki website ssc.nic.in pe aata hai.
