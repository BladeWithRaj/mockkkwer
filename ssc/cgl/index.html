SSC CGL Preparation Tips

Top strategies for SSC CGL 2026

Time Management

Allocate maximum 15 minutes per section. Solve easy questions first, attempt difficult ones later. Practice with a timer consistently.

Accuracy Over Speed

With -0.50 negative marking, guessing is risky. Correctly solving 70-80 questions is better than attempting all 100 with low accuracy.

Daily Mock Tests

Take one mock test daily. Review analysis, identify weak areas, and focus on those topics. Consistency is the key to cracking SSC CGL.

SSC CGL Mock Test — FAQ

SSC CGL ke liye candidate ka graduate (any discipline) hona zaroori hai kisi recognized university se. Age limit 18-32 years hai (category ke hisaab se relaxation milta hai). Indian citizenship zaroori hai.
SSC CGL Tier-1 cutoff category-wise alag hota hai. General category ke liye typically 140-155/200 ke beech rehta hai. OBC ke liye ~130-140, SC ke liye ~110-120. Yeh har saal question difficulty pe depend karta hai.
SSC CGL ab 2 main tiers mein hota hai: Tier-1 (Computer Based, 100 Qs, 60 min — qualifying + merit), Tier-2 (Computer Based, Session I + II — merit deciding). DEST & CPT skill tests hain specific posts ke liye.
SSC CGL se Group B aur C posts bhare jaate hain: Income Tax Inspector, CBI Sub-Inspector, Auditor, Accountant, Assistant, UDC (Upper Division Clerk), etc. Saare central government departments mein posting hoti hai.
Hamare mock tests SSC CGL ke exact pattern pe based hain — 100 questions, 60 min, 4 sections with 25 questions each, -0.50 negative marking. Questions ka difficulty level bhi real exam ke comparable hai.
