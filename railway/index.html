❓ Railway Exam FAQ

RRB NTPC CBT-1 mein 100 questions hote hain 90 minutes mein. 3 sections: Mathematics (30), General Intelligence & Reasoning (30), General Awareness (40). Har galat answer pe 1/3 marks katate hain.
NTPC graduate level posts ke liye hai (Station Master, Clerk, etc.) jabki Group D 10th pass ke liye hai (Helper, Porter, etc.). Group D mein General Science bhi aata hai jo NTPC mein nahi hai.
