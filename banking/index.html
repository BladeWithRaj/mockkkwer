❓ Banking Exam FAQ

IBPS PO se public sector banks (PNB, BOB, Canara etc.) mein posting hoti hai, jabki SBI PO specifically State Bank of India mein. Pattern same hai lekin SBI ka difficulty thoda zyada hota hai.
Almost saari banking prelims mein 100 questions hote hain 60 minutes mein — Quantitative Aptitude (35), Reasoning (35), English (30). -0.25 negative marking hoti hai.
